Saturday, 11 July, 2009



jolly as a bee.



What's good, stranger?



Today, I was at Macy's on the Vegas Strip, and one of your kind came up to me and took me by
surprise.



I was walking around Fashion Show Mall with my brother, and ended up spending a bunch of time at
Macy's. Looking around, I found this nice suit vest thingy (or I guess waistcoat thinger, if
you speak non-Canadian Commonwealth English). I tried it on in front of a mirror, but I decided
it wasn't that great. Hanger in hand, I walked a ways back to where I found it, and as I was about to
put it back this guy says " Excuse me " and I turn around. He's pretty well dressed, and has a pair
of those large, vintage glasses that are starting to pop up everywhere (and that, I have to
reluctantly admit, I would like to get myself [Read: Oh no, is this the beginning of a hipster
?]). Anyways, he asks me " Do you work here? " and I reply with " No, sorry " and then he says, " Oh...
you look stylish enough to work here. " And then walks away.



Whaaaaat? That was pretty cool, ahahah.



I put away the vest, and I think about what I'm wearing. Clean, simple, but it was probably the
keffiyeh (eurgh, yeah... whatever). I look around for some actual employees, and realize
that I sort of look like them, more or less. Okay, sure. I take it as a compliment.





Today was nice... went around the Strip again, and found some surprisingly good bubble tea and
Japanese fast food. Really good. Christ, I love eating. I do it as a hobby.





Image: http://img11.imageshack.us/img11/9202/img00222z.jpg Slushie bubble tea beats
the heat.



Yesterday, though. OH GEEZE. I sort of was in a happy daze all of yesterday evening, since for
the first time in my 18 years alive as a Filipino person I finally ate at a Jollibee for the very
first time. It was REALLY good, and I ordered A LOT of food.



What is Jollibee, you ask? It's the McDonald's of Southeast Asia . Except very much less
despised and far more not un-nutritious. Actually, it's the only fast-food chain that has
been able to outdo Mickey D's anywhere in the world. To be completely honest, if there was any
reason for me to have come to Las Vegas, it would be to finally have some Jollibee.





Image: http://img194.imageshack.us/img194/2415/dsc08577.jpg Chickenjoy, rice, Jolly
Crispy Fries, "Heavyweight" Yumburger, sweet (Filipino-style) spaghetti, Peach Mango
Pie. Too good.

I was actually surprised to learn that it was the first time my Filipino parents had Jollibee
too... it was formed in the Philippines after they immigrated to the middle of nowhere, Canada
more than two decades ago.





That day, we also went to Hoover Dam , among other things (like a Filipino convention at South
Point, the Strip again, etc.). We took a tour, and it was really interesting and fun, actually.
I kept thinking about the Transformers movie .





Image: http://img36.imageshack.us/img36/8461/dsc08491c.jpg



Image: http://img16.imageshack.us/img16/6801/dsc08511a.jpg

man-made wonders of the world.





Image: http://img36.imageshack.us/img36/6537/dsc08515.jpg Just chillen in hoover dam
no biggie, lulz.



Image: http://img16.imageshack.us/img16/6674/dsc08542u.jpg WTF, stop it.



Needless to say, yesterday was a very good day for me.

Asian-style. No fear... NO SHAME, or as the Filipino would say...
