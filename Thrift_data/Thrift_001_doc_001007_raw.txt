Photo: Lens Flare Ahoy

I'm surprised this photo turned out, so close to the sun; my old camera probably would have been
overwhelmed by the light, but my new one was capable enough to just make lens flares. Here, I've
caught a Bombardier CRJ200 flying for US Airways Express descending into Phoenix Sky Harbor
International Airport on the sort of blue sky day that's common for the desert in January.
