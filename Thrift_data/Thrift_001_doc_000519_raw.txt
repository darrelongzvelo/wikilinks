Mayfair Wellington FL Homes For Sale



Homes for sale in Mayfair offer active adults in Wellington FL a care free lifestyle. Publix
grocery store is within walking distance along with CVS Pharmacy and the Village of
Wellington Community Center . Restaurants, entertainment and additional shopping is also
close by.





Mayfair in Wellington FL was built by Engle Homes from 2006 to 2007. There are only 112 homes
within the gates of Mayfair and two different styles were built, Coach Homes and Carriage
Homes.



The Coach Series in Mayfair has two different floorplans with a living square footage of 1,716
and 1,95. Each Coach home in Mayfair has a two car garage with additional driveway parking.



The Carriage Series in Mayfair also has two different floorplans with a living square footage
of 1,550 and 1,852. Each Carriage home in Mayfair has a one car garage with additional driveway
parking.



Almost all of the homes in Mayfair are located on water, either Lake Wellington, a peaceful
canal leading out to Lake Wellington or the beautiful lake located in the center of Mayfair.
