Chennai Superstars Logo

Chennai Superstars is one of the teams playing in the Indian Cricket League (ICL) founded in
2007. The team is named after the popular superstar of Tamilnadu , Rajnikanth . The team is
based in Chennai , coached by Michael Bevan and captained by Stuart Law , both reputed
Australian cricketers. The team has won the first two championships of Indian Cricket League
, the ICL Inaugural T20 held in 2007 and the ICL 50 Overs Tournament held in 2008.
