Tabebuia caraiba ..........................................Yellow Poui, Silver
Trumpet Tree - Flowers profusely in late spring and early summer (May - June). Bloom is golden
                                                                             
     yellow, with thousands of flowers showing on each tree. Foliage is dropped just prior
to blooming, with new growth                                                      
                               coming in towards the end of the blooming period. Legend
has it that when a yellow poui is in very heavy bloom, that it a                            
                                                precursor to an active hurricane
season ! Plant in full sun.

Thyme ............................................................. Thymus
vulgaris - EDIBLE. Perennial herb. Used as a general spice in sauces, cooking, dressings.
Full sun or part                                                                  
                  shade.

Tarragon   Russian ......................................... Artemisia dracunculus -
EDIBLE. Perennial herb. Used in sauces, cooking, wines, teas, and as a general            
                                                                             
                                              spice. Full sun or part shade. 

Ti Plant cultivars .............................................. Cordyline
terminalis 'Black Magic' - Many varieties available; 'Cotton Candy' - light pink, 'Black
Magic' - almost black,                                                            
                           'Red Sister' - most popular, reddish leaf, many others. Used
for foliage and color accents.

Talinum paniculatum variegatum ................Jewel of Opar - A low growing shrub, white
and green leaves. Small pink flowers. Drought tolerant.





Tecoma stans .................................................Yellow Elder -
National Flower of The Bahamas . Bright yellow, trumpet shaped flowers borne in clusters on
new growth.                                                                    
          Light green foliage, serrated, elongated. Growth habit is somewhat sprawling,
can be trained into a single trunked tree,                                            
                                   prefers to grow as a shrub. Best when left unpruned!
Let 'em go!

Texas Wild Olive ............................................ Cordia boissieri , White
Geiger Tree - Growth to 15 or 25 feet. Large leaf, white flower, profuse bloomer. Medium salt  
                                                                             
   tolerance. Can be trained to very interesting shapes.

Thryallis .......................................................... Galphimia
glauca , Shower-of-Gold - Bright yellow flowers on a small leaf shrub. Flowers most of the
year. Use as a                                                                    
                 hedge, shrub, or in containers.

Thunbergia grandiflora ................................. Purple Bengal Clock Vine, Sky
Vine - A climbing vine, with large, light purple flowers. Requires high amounts of nutrients,
                                                                           a
supplement of iron (Fe) several times a year is very beneficial.





Thunbergia grandiflora 'alba'......................... White Bengal Clock Vine - As
above, with white flowers.

Tillandsias .....................................................Tillandsia -
assorted varieties available, bareroot, often flowering.

Trachelospermum asiaticum .....................Asiatic Jasmine - Very low growing
groundcover, horizontal growth habit. Small dark green foliage, small white flowers.



Trachelospermum jasminoides ..................Confederate Jasmine - FRAGRANT. Small
deep green foliage, with small (3/4" diameter) white flowers, borne in small              
                                                                   clusters.
More of a climbing variety than T. asiaticum with vertical growth habit, great for trellises
or lattice work.

White Geiger Tree .......................................... Cordia boissieri , Texas
Wild Olive - Growth to 15 or 25 feet. Large leaf, white flower, profuse bloomer. Medium salt  
                                                                             
      tolerance.

Traveller's Palm ............................................. Ravenala
madascariensis - A large growing tree, similar in appearance to a Bird of Paradise or Banana,
but larger.                                                                    
                 Crown is somewhat flat, leaves grow in one direction.

Tree Crinum .................................................... Crinum asiaticum ,
Poison Bulb, Crinum Lily - Salt tolerant. Light green strap leaves. Also available in RED.
White flowers.

Triangle Palm ................................................ Dypsis decaryi - Crown
grows in a triangular shape. Use in full sun or part shade, prefers some moisture.          
                                                                             
           Slow growth.



 



Uniola paniculata ...........................................Sea Oats - NATIVE.
PROTECTED. Grows on sand dunes, used in dune reclamation. SALT tolerant. Great for        
                                                                             
      mass plantings. Spreads by runners.







Variegated Ginger ......................................... Alpinia zerumbet
variegata - Yellow and green striped foliage. Prefers some moisture and light shade.

Variegated Ginger White Stripe ................... Alpinia sanderae 'White Stripe' - Many
small white stripes on leaf. Prefers a moist, lighty shaded location.

Variegated Liriope ......................................... Liriope muscari
'Variegata' , Aztec Grass - Light green with white stripes, grass-like strap leaves. Growth
to about 24                                                                      
            inches when healthy. Prefers full sun or part shade. Do not over-water.

Variegated Shefflera ...................................... Schefflera arboricola
'Trinette', or 'Gold Capella' - Salt tolerant shrubs, with green and yellow variegated
foliage. Can be                                                                  
              pruned to shape. Full sun or part shade.



Vinca ............................................................... Catharanus
roseus - Medicinal. Flowers white, pink, lavender, some hybrids have red colors. Older
varieties are hardier                                                            
                   than new hybrids. Perennial.

Vines : Allamanda cathartica .......................Yellow, Common Allamanda - 4" golden
yellow flower. Wonderful vine for a fence or a wall.

Vines: Bleeding Heart .................................... Clerodendrum thomsoniae - A
climbing vine, with white and red flowers borne in clusters. Deep green leaf.

Vines: Blue Passion Flower .......................... Passiflora caerulea - Fast growing
vine with blue/purple flowers, very intricate. Use in containers on trellises, on        
                                                                             
   fences, walls, trees, etc.

Vines: Confederate Jasmine ........................ Trachelospermum jasminoides -
FRAGRANT. Small deep green foliage, with small (3/4" diameter) white flowers, borne in    
                                                                        small
clusters. More of a climbing variety than T. asiaticum with vertical growth habit, great    
                                                                             
                                     for trellises or lattice work.

Vines: Garlic Vine .......................................... Mansoa alliacea -
Purple/Violet flowers on a rambling vine. Foliage is scented of garlic. Flowers much of the  
                                                                             
              year. Great for fences or trellises.

Vines: Madagascar Jasmine ........................ Stephanotis floribunda - FRAGRANT. A
slow growing vine, deep green foliage, small tubular flower, white. Great for            
                                                                       
arbours, trellises.

Vines: Passion Flower Red .......................... Passiflora coccinea - Fast growing
vine with red flowers, very intricate. Use in containers on trellises, on                
                                                                             
     fences, walls, trees, etc.

Vines: Passion Fruit ...................................... Passiflora edulis - Fast
growing vine, similar flower to Blue Passion Flower, followed by an EDIBLE fruit, several  
                                                                             
         varieties available. Gorgeous fruit for toppings, beverages, iced cream etc.

Vines: Queen's Wreath ................................. Petrea volubilis - A climbing
vine, leaves are textured like sandpaper. Flower is purple, drooping in clusters,        
                                                                             
         similar to Wisteria in the north. Attracts BEES, HUMMINGBIRDS, and BIRDS.

Washingtonia Palm ..................................... Washingtonia robusta - A
large, fast growing palm, up to 80 feet in 20 years. Very strong and wind tolerant.



Wax Begonia ................................................. Begonia x
semperflorens - cultorum , Annual Begonia - Mostly used as bedding plants in the winter
season. Can be                                                                  
                    used in containers. Waxy, shiny foliage, greens and burgundy colors,
                                                                             
                                                                       white,
red, pink, etc. Many cultivars.



White Bird of Paradise ................................. Strelitzia nicolai - Growth to
approx. 10 or 15 feet. Large white bloom. Banana-like foliage.

White Butterfly Nephthytis .............................. Syngonium podophyllum
'White Butterfly' - Foliage is white and green. Spreading, used in groundcover or in
containers.                                                                    
           Sun or shade.

White Ixora 'Frankie Hipp' .............................White Ixora - White flowering
variety. Large growth habit, high nutritional requirements and pest control requirements.

White Ixora miniature 'Diora' .........................Miniature Ixora - White
flowering miniature Ixora. Needs high amounts of nutrients and pest control. Prefers good
soil.

White Mandevilla ........................................... Mandevilla 'Sun
Parasol' - Large white flower with a yellow center. Vining plant best used on a trellis or
support.

Wodyetia bifurcata ........................................Foxtail Palm - A medium
sized palm, graceful, feathery foliage, shaped like a foxtail. Full sun or part            
                                                                             
               shade, even moisture.

Xanadu ........................................................... Philodendron '
Xanadu ' - VERY HIGH VALUE landscape plant. Deeply lobed foliage up to 6" length, 4" width.
Clumping                                                                      
          habit, can get to 4 feet in diameter or more. Low growing. Can be testy if
mistreated.

, Silver Trumpet Tree - Flowers profusely in late spring and early summer (May - June). Bloom is
golden                                                                        
    yellow, with thousands of flowers showing on each tree. Foliage is dropped just prior to
blooming, with new growth

Yellow Allamanda ........................................... Allamanda cathartica -
4" golden yellow flower. Wonderful vine for a fence or a wall.

Yesterday , Today, and Tomorrow ............... Brunfelsia australis - Bloom starts as
Purple 1st day, Pink 2nd day, and White 3rd day, hence the name.

Ylang Ylang ..................................................... Cananga odorata -
Very FRAGRANT. Not common. Growth up to 30 feet or more.

Yucca elaphantipes .......................................Yucca - Upright growth
habit, to 15 feet or more. Foliage is thin (1 1/2") and long (30"), spiked at the tip. Flowers
from the                                                                        
   top of the plant, once a year, not every year. Beautiful, white flowers, hundreds, on a
stalk 2 or 3 feet long.





Vanda Orchid .................................................Vanda hybrids - Ground
orchids and regular Vanda hybrids available. Many colors.

Tulbaghia violacea .......................................Society Garlic - A small
(18") grass-like plant, part of the Allium (onion) family, scented foliage. Flower is pink,
small,                                                                        
         raised above the foliage. Clumping growth habit.

Vines: Bengal Clock Vine, Sky Vine ............ Thunbergia grandiflora - A climbing vine,
with large, light purple flowers. Requires high amounts of nutrients, a                  
                                                                         
supplement of iron (Fe) several times a year is very beneficial.
