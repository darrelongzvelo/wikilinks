Relationships

Image: /sites/default/files/imagecache/medium/rishi%20and%20neetu%20kapoor.jpg

On the 6th of May, 2012, one of Bollywood's golden couple, Rishi and Neetu Kapoor will be
inaugarating the Indian Cinema In Conversation five part series being held at the Powerade
Centre in Brampton .

The couple will talk about their insights into the world of Bollywood and how they have managed
to stay grounded and together, unlike other Bollywood couples whose marriages have not
lasted very long.

Rishi Kapoor will speak about his experience as an Actor/Producer/Director and how his
journey has been in fulfilling each of these roles sucessfully. Neetu Kapoor who had taken a
hiatus from the film industry for a very long time has returned to the silver screen with her
husband in " Do Dooni Char ."

In order to exeprience this fun filled insightful evening, tickets can be purchased in the
following manner:

Online- Ticketmaster - www.ticketmaster.ca

In person- Powerade Centre Box Office in Brampton located at 7575 Kennedy Road S.
