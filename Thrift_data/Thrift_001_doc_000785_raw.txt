Trout Haven, Knapp Creek WI - TROUT SEASON HAS ARRIVED!



'Knapp Creek'  and ' Trout Haven'  are a hidden jewel of an oasis located only 85 miles
from ' Minnesota's Twin Cities metro' , with Northwoods Ambience bar none. You will catch
your abundance of Brook Trout , Rainbow Trout  and Brown Trout from this spring-fed trout
stream, strategically located downstream from a trout hatchery. Whether you are an
experienced angler or a novice there is a fly-casting instructor and guide available for '
Trout Haven' residents and ' Trout Haven' guests.



You will lose your sense of time and space in these 29 beautiful, wooded acres. Be still and let
the magic of the sound of running water envelope you and fill you with peace and a sense of well
being. And what is important will begin to make sense.

Mindy Stolts's Trout Fishing Tips

Wisconsin's trout fishing season runs from March to Mid-November.



    Some of the basics a beginner would want to know are:

    - How to select and purchase the essential fly fishing equipment like fly rods , reels ,
    line , waders and fishing vests .

    

    - Most commonly used flies, including dry, nymph and streamers.

    

    - How to tie the basic knots

    

    - How to cast

    If I was to give advice to the seasoned fly fisherperson it would be to not limit yourself
    to lakes and streams but, be open to the challenge of ocean and backwater fly fishing. It
    can be a satisfying challenge .

 -3 fabulous golf courses within 3 miles

-McKenzie Creek wildlife area

-Chateau St Croix Winery

-Live Festival Theatre

-Spring Maple tree tapping

And much much more!
