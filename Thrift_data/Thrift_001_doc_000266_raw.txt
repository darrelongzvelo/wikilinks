What are some of the indigenous wild animals that typically live in your country?

The indigenous wild animals in Spain are snakes, wild cats, pigs, donkeys, birds, bulls,
sheep, wild pigs, wolves, and wild dogs.



What are some of the farm animals that typically live in your country?

The farm animals are cow, chicken, sheep, and pig.



What are some of the pets that typically live in your country?

Pets in Spain are dogs, cats, and fish.



Assignment #5 What is your Spain famous for?



Why is Spain famous for bull fight?

Spain is famous for bull fight because every year Spanish people organize a contest for bull
fighting.



What is a bull fight?

A bull fight is a sport in which a man kills the bull after exhausting the bull by avoiding its
attack with a red piece of cloth, because the red color excites the bull. The fighter then will
thrust a sword and kill the bull. It is a blood sport, some countries do not allow the killing of
the bull.



What do they use a bull fight for?

They use it for entertainment and it is a sport.



Where do you find bull fights?

You find bull fights in Spain, Portugal , some cities in southern France and in several Latin
American countries such as Mexico and Peru.



Who invented bull fight?

The Spanish introduced the practice of fighting on foot around 1726. Francisco Romero is
generally regarded as having been the first to do this.
