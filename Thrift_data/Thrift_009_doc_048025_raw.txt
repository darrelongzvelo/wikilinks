Quote: Originally Posted by Wikipedia Freak folk is a genre of folk music associated with
contemporary artists such as Faun Fables , Animal Collective , Devendra Banhart , CocoRosie ,
Panda Bear , Joanna Newsom , Sufjan Stevens , Bowerbirds , Woods , Greg Weeks , Akron/Family ,
Rio en Medio , Ariel Pink and with artists of the 1960s and 1970s such as The Incredible String
Band , Vashti Bunyan , Holy Modal Rounders , The Godz , and The Fugs .





Freak folk draws from traditional folk music and uses mainly acoustic instrumentation, but
introduces elements of avant-garde music , baroque pop and psychedelic folk , often
featuring uncommon sounds, themes, and vocal styles. Freak folk - Wikipedia, the free
encyclopedia
