Bay City Rollers -(Bye Bye Baby) The Very Best Of The Bay City Rollers. CD





The Bay City Rollers were a Scottish pop/rock band of the 1970s. Their youthful, clean-cut
image, distinctive styling featuring tartan -trimmed outfits, and cheery, sing-along pop
hits helped the group become among the most popular musical acts of their time. For a
relatively brief but fervent period (nicknamed "Rollermania"), they were worldwide teen
idols . The group's line-up featured numerous changes over the years, but the classic line-up
during its heyday included guitarists Eric Faulkner and Stuart Wood , singer Les McKeown ,
bassist Alan Longmuir , and drummer Derek Longmuir .

Since the band's quick rise to, and subsequent fall from fame, the members have endured
numerous and varied struggles regarding royalty payments, substance abuse, and personal
legal problems.
